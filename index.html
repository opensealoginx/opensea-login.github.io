What do I need to get started with OpenSea?


OpenSea login is  a browser-based service that may be used on a desktop or mobile device.  You’ll need a mobile wallet or a wallet extension to create an account.  Ethereum compatibility is also required for mobile browsers. To get  started, you’ll need to fund your wallet with cryptocurrency.


When trading at OpenSea, what method of payment do I use?


Payments on OpenSea are often bitcoins since items are published on a  blockchain ledger. The most popular payment option on OpenSea is Ether.  However, they accept over 240 other payment methods. As some groups  have payment mechanisms, various social and gaming currencies are used.


What Should I Do If I Want To Mint At OpenSea?


Minting is simple and gas-free at OpenSea.  You can make an NFT in minutes and without writing any code. Choose  ‘make a collection,’ ‘add an item to the collection,’ fill in the  information, and then select ‘create.’ You’re ready to go after listing  the item for sale.
